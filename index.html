Why Fassah Exists
"Salah is the first thing accounted for on the Day of Judgement."

Every Muslim knows this. Yet across the UK, millions of prayers are missed — not from neglect, but from circumstance. You're travelling. You're at university. You're between appointments in an unfamiliar part of the city. The adhan sounds and you simply don't know where to go.

Fassah exists because that moment should never be the reason a prayer is lost. Built by the community, for the community. Every space you add is sadaqah jariyah — reward for everyone who ever prays there.
